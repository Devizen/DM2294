---------------DW(Dynamic Warrior)-------------------------
This is a guide to the controls of our game, DW.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Brief description~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Player is suppose to destroy towers in each level to proceed
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Movement~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
>'W' - Move forward
>'S' - Move backwards
>'A' - Move left
>'D' - Move right
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Camera Movement~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
>Mouse

**OR**

>Keyboard
 >Left arrow  -rotate camera to left
 >Right arrow -rotate camera to right
 >Up arrow    -rotate camera to up
 >Down arrow  -rotate camera to down
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Other controls~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
>Left mouse click  - Fire weapon
>Right mouse click - Melee attack(6th consecutive melee does a special melee attack)
>Space             - Jump
>left Ctrl         - change posture from Stand up->crouch->Prone->Stand up respectively
>'Q'/Scroll up     - Change weapon
>'M'               - Open enlarged minimap
>Tab               - Locks on to nearest enemy
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Inventory~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
>'W' - Cycle Up
>'S' - Cycle Down
>'A' - Cycle Left
>'D' - Cycle Right
>'I' - Access inventory 
>'E' - Equip item
>'F' - Discard item
>'U' - Close inventory
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Shop~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
>'W' - Cycle Up
>'S' - Cycle Down
>'A' - Cycle Left
>'D' - Cycle Right
>'F' - Access Buy Menu 
>'B' - Buy item highlighted
>'U' - Close inventory
>'E' - Access Equipment
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Equipment~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
>'W' - Cycle Up
>'S' - Cycle Down
>'A' - Cycle Left
>'D' - Cycle Right
>'Q' - Unequip item
>'U' - Close inventory
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Map Editor(Developer use)~~~~~~~~~~~~~
>'7'      - Open map editor
>'9'      - Close map editor
>'4'      - Choose enemies
	      - Cycle left
>'6'      - Choose map objects
	      - Cycle Right
>'1'      - Scale down
>'3'      - Scale up
>'8'      - Rotate enemy/object
>'5'      - Place chosen enemy/object
>'0'      - Remove most recently placed enemy/object
>Left Alt - Change scale axis
>'N'      - Saves map items and enemies
